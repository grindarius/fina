What is this site?

This site is called FINA. It's basically a calculator for Numerical Analysis subject.

What are the motivations behind this website?

During winter of 2020. I have to take this course. It's a course about basically every mathematics thing I could find. And the teacher that's teaching this subject is just not the best, it makes be bored. But one day while I was doing the homework. I see how the Bisection method is doing its job repetitively. So I thought why don't I create a program to automate it. So I spent that day, about 2 hours, code the Bisection method prototype program. And that's the start of the journey of FINA.
